Uncontrollable urge to shop and spend? Don't know where your money go? Need to keep track of your financial? MyFinance here to help! Your digital solution to help with your financial.

We offer,
1. MyTracker to keep records of your incomes and expenses
2. Transaction history to help view past incomes and/or expenses effortlessly
3. Report & Insight that calculate and analyze your financial
4. Cloud-Saving to keep your data secure and accesible from any device
5. Local Account to keep your finance fully private
6. Offline access with local account
7. Install and access without opening browser
